## ğŸ¯ Best Practices

### For CV Optimization

1. **Be Specific**: Use the exact job description
2. **Quality Over Quantity**: Better to use Smart/Full fix on one targeted CV than Basic on many
3. **Customize**: Even after AI optimization, add personal touches
4. **Keywords Matter**: Make sure industry-specific terms are included
5. **Proofread**: Always do a final check before sending

### For Cover Letters

1. **Research**: Know the company before generating
2. **Personalize**: Edit the AI-generated letter with specific examples
3. **Keep It Short**: One page maximum
4. **Call to Action**: End with enthusiasm and next steps
5. **Match Your CV**: Ensure cover letter aligns with your CV

### For Interview Prep

1. **Practice Early**: Don't wait until the day before
2. **Record Yourself**: Practice answers on video
3. **Focus on STAR**: Situation, Task, Action, Result
4. **Prepare Questions**: Have 3-5 questions ready to ask
5. **Research Company**: Know their recent news and achievements

### For Application Tracking

1. **Update Immediately**: Log applications right after sending
2. **Be Detailed**: Add notes about job requirements, salary, etc.
3. **Follow Up**: Set reminders to check in with companies
4. **Learn from Rejections**: Note patterns to improve future applications
